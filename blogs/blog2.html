Importance of SEO for Small Business – Learn to Improve Your Website

Akshit Bansal

SEO a.k.a Search Engine Optimization is an essential element of digital marketing where it acts as the backbone to a website. It makes a website stronger with the implementation of various keywords, which describes the business in detail. Keywords are nothing but words or sets of words that describe your business, hence giving it a perfect framework.

In this blog, we’ll talk more about Off-Page SEO, which can enhance your website’s opportunity to increase its traffic, attract more customers, and eventually, increase your brand awareness effectively – it is all about creating quality content and links!

“Did You Know?
SEO drives 1000%+ more traffic than organic social media. (BrightEdge)”

Let’s take an example to understand more. Suppose you have a business that sells fresh vegetables and fruits. In that case, your primary keywords will be ‘fresh farm vegetables and fruits,’ ‘fresh vegetables and fruits,’ ‘farm to table,’ ‘organic vegetables and fruits’ and more. Your secondary keywords will be the unique selling points (USPs) like ‘free home delivery,’ ‘home delivery available,’ ‘state/country name,’ ‘combo offer,’ and more. The primary and secondary keywords give a proper, yet the complete view of your business. When a user types’ fresh vegetables in Meerut’ your website appears on the search engines. However, the ranking depends on the keyword optimization done on your website. A website with proper keyword optimization along with the content, can affect your ranking. If you are on the first page of the top 10 Google Search Engine Results Page (SERP), you have done the job right!

Now that you have understood the importance of SEO, let’s see how you can implement it on your website. Go through the blog word-by-word to understand more. If you are running a small business, remember SEO plays a super-crucial role. It may be time consuming, affordable, and organic yet exceptionally useful.

The Right Way to Optimize Your Website

First of all, before we begin, you need to understand that SEO is an umbrella term and contains two basic categories – On-Page SEO and Off-Page SEO .

On-Page SEO

On-page SEO is all about working on your website to make it more readable for the audience. Again, it is all about keyword optimization. Each webpage of your website needs five essential elements which are as follows:-

  1. SEO Title
  2. Meta Description
  3. Page Content
  4. Image Optimization
SEO Title

A page title determines the true essence of the webpage. The title must contain the keyword and also, should explain about the respective webpage.

For example, suppose you are running a farm, and you are selling ‘fresh farm vegetables and fruits‘ or ‘Vegetables Near Me’. Then your page title can be ‘Fresh Farm Vegetables and Fruits from Farm| Meerut,’ or ‘Farm Fresh Organic Vegetables & Fruits – FREE HOME DELIVERY‘ and more.

There are two most important things to remember. First, try to keep the title within its character limits, i.e. 50 – 60 characters only. Secondly, add the page title in the page content as H1 tag.

“Did You Know?
69.7% of search queries contain four words or more. (Ahrefs)”

Meta Description

A page description explains further about your page title. It gives the user the reason to click the link and further explore.

For example, your page description can be ‘Get fresh farm vegetables and fruits from us in Meerut. Our products are chemical-free, hence giving you a healthy choice,’ or ‘Get fresh farm vegetables and fruits at your doorstep – FREE HOME DELIVERY available above ₹ 500! Buy our chemical-free and organically grown produce’ .

You can add only your primary or both primary and secondary keywords in the product description, but remember, keep it short and simple, yet informative but only in 50 – 160 characters.

Page Content

The page content is the detail explanation of the webpage. It needs to have the right flow of words that is user and reader-friendly. Do not overload the content with challenging vocabulary. Understand your audience and go ahead and create content on that understanding.

Google loves a webpage with rich content with the right keyword optimization. You can have the content between 500-100 and even more words. Remember, the quality matters here!

The other factor that matters is the keyword density. Your keyword density should always be between 1-2% (Keyword Density = Keywords/Total Words * 100). For example, if your content is around 800 words with keywords repeating 17X, then 15/800*100 = 1.81%. You should always measure the keyword density to avoid keyword stuffing, which affects your SEO and Google Penalty will take your website down entirely.

Image Optimization

Image optimization is one of the essential components of SEO, yet many companies ignore it. Every image on the web page should be optimized with the right set of keywords, starting from the banner, side images, info-graphics, to even videos.

A user when searching for the keywords in the ‘image search’ can also end up at the website, hence, increasing your website traffic. So, image optimization is vital for SEO too.

The most important part is to maintain a healthy image dimension. If the images are heavy, it’ll affect the site’s speed, hence, increasing your bounce rate.

“Did You Know?
On average, around 64% of a website’s weight is comprised of images (HTTP Archive)”

Off-Page SEO

Off-Page SEO is a secondary, yet an important part of digital marketing. It is all about actions taken particularly outside the website which impacts your website ranking deeply, and back-links are the vital part of it.
